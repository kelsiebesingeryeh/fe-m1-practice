Plant-Based Cooking

About Me


Hi! My name is Kelsie Besinger Yeh and I've been a vegetarian for the last 15+ years! Recently my husband and I have gotten into plant-based recipes and cooking vegan meals. We both love the feeling of eating healthy and also helping the environment. We have a lot of fun coming up with recipes and following vegan bloggers. Vegan food is really fun to explore and super delicious!

Denver Vegan Food Guide

Moving to Denver from NYC was definitely a lifestyle adjustment, but the saddest part of leaving was the food scene! There were so many vegan options in New York, however there are quite a few great vegan options here in Denver. I've listed below my favorite restaurants with vegan options!

  • Watercourse Foods (100% vegan)
  • Vital Root (99% vegan)
  • Native Foods (100% vegan)
